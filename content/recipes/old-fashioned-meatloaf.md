## Ingredients

### Core
- Ground beef
- Breadcrumbs
- Egg
- Onion, minced
- Milk

### Glaze
- Ketchup
- Brown sugar
- Worcestershire sauce

### Seasoning
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Garlic powder

---

## Instructions

### Mix
- Combine all loaf ingredients in a bowl.
- Do not overwork.

### Shape
- Form into a loaf in a baking dish.

### Glaze
- Mix glaze ingredients and spread on top.

### Bake
- Bake at 350°F until cooked through.

---

## Overseer’s Notes
- Leftovers make excellent sandwiches.
- Add grated carrot or zucchini for moisture.
- Freezes well.

## Pairings
- Mashed potatoes
- Green beans
- Dinner rolls
