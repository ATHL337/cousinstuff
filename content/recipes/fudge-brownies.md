## Ingredients

### Core
- Unsalted butter
- Granulated sugar
- Brown sugar
- Eggs
- Vanilla extract
- All-purpose flour
- Cocoa powder
- Salt

### Add-Ins (Optional)
- Chocolate chips
- Chopped walnuts

---

## Instructions

### Melt Base
- Melt butter and whisk with sugars.

### Add Eggs
- Whisk in eggs and vanilla.

### Dry In
- Stir in flour, cocoa, and salt.

### Bake
- Spread in lined pan.
- Bake at 350°F until set but soft in the center.

---

## Overseer’s Notes
- Underbake slightly for fudgier texture.
- Chill before slicing for clean edges.
- Excellent base for sundaes.

## Pairings
- Vanilla ice cream
- Cold milk
- Fresh strawberries
