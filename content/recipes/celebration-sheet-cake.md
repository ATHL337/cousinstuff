## Ingredients

### Cake
- All-purpose flour
- Granulated sugar
- Baking powder
- Baking soda
- Salt
- Eggs
- Milk
- Vegetable oil
- Vanilla extract

### Frosting
- Unsalted butter, softened
- Powdered sugar
- Heavy cream or milk
- Vanilla extract

### Optional Toppings
- Sprinkles
- Chocolate shavings
- Fresh berries

---

## Instructions

### Mix Batter
- Whisk dry ingredients in a large bowl.
- Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla.
- Mix until smooth.

### Bake
- Pour into a greased sheet pan.
- Bake at 350°F until a toothpick comes out clean.

### Frost
- Beat butter until fluffy.
- Gradually add powdered sugar and cream.
- Spread generously once cake cools.

### Decorate
- Add toppings if desired.

---

## Overseer’s Notes
- This is a “feed a crowd” dessert.
- Flavor variations: chocolate, lemon, funfetti.
- Keeps well covered for 2–3 days.

## Pairings
- Coffee
- Punch
- Ice cream
