## Ingredients

### Core
- Vanilla ice cream
- Milk
- Chocolate syrup

### Optional (Adults Only)
- Root beer schnapps
- Irish cream
- Vanilla vodka

### Topping
- Whipped cream
- Sprinkles
- Maraschino cherries

---

## Instructions

### Blend
- Add all ingredients to blender.
- Blend until smooth.

### Serve
- Pour into tall glasses.
- Top generously.
