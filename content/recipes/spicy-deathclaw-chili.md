## Ingredients

### Core
- Ground beef
- Onion, diced
- Garlic, minced
- Canned crushed tomatoes
- Kidney or black beans

### Seasoning
- Chili powder
- Cumin
- Smoked paprika
- Cayenne
- Salt
- Black pepper

---

## Instructions

### Brown
- Brown beef with onion and garlic.

### Build
- Add tomatoes, beans, and spices.

### Simmer
- Simmer uncovered 45 minutes.

### Adjust
- Add salt and heat to taste.
