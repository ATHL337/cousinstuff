## Ingredients

### Core
- Lump crab meat
- Mayonnaise
- Dijon mustard
- Egg
- Breadcrumbs

### Seasoning & Spices
- Old Bay or Cajun seasoning
- Worcestershire sauce
- Garlic powder
- Black pepper
- Paprika

### For Cooking
- Neutral oil or butter

---

## Instructions

### Combine
- In a large bowl, mix mayo, mustard, egg, Worcestershire, and spices.
- Gently fold in crab and breadcrumbs.

### Form
- Shape into patties.
- Chill 10 minutes to firm.

### Sear
- Heat oil in skillet over medium.
- Cook 3–4 minutes per side until golden.

### Serve
- Drain briefly and serve hot.

---

## Overseer’s Notes
- Do not overmix; texture matters.
- Chill prevents collapse.
- Excellent with lemon aioli or remoulade.
