## Ingredients

### Crust
- All-purpose flour
- Powdered sugar
- Butter

### Filling
- Eggs
- Granulated sugar
- Fresh lemon juice
- Lemon zest
- Flour

---

## Instructions

### Bake Crust
- Mix crust ingredients.
- Press into pan.
- Bake until lightly golden.

### Mix Filling
- Whisk eggs, sugar, lemon juice, zest, and flour.

### Bake Again
- Pour over crust.
- Bake until set.

### Finish
- Cool completely.
- Dust with powdered sugar.

---

## Overseer’s Notes
- Chill for cleaner slices.
- Great for potlucks.
- Can be frozen.
