## Ingredients

### Core
- Chicken (bones/carcass or thighs)
- Onion
- Carrots
- Celery
- Garlic
- Bay leaf

### Seasoning
- Salt
- Pepper

---

## Instructions

### Simmer
- Cover ingredients with water
- Bring to gentle simmer

### Cook
- Simmer 90–120 min

### Strain
- Strain and season

### Store
- Cool quickly
- Refrigerate or freeze

---

## Overseer’s Notes
- Freeze in 2-cup portions for fast deployments.
- Great base for VT soups.
