## Ingredients

### Core
- 2–3 pounds chicken bones, wings, or carcasses
- 10 cups water

### Aromatics
- 1 onion, quartered
- 2 carrots, chopped
- 2 celery stalks, chopped
- 4 cloves garlic, smashed

### Seasoning
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 tsp black peppercorns
- Salt, to taste

---

## Instructions

### Load Reactor
- Add all ingredients to a large stockpot.
- Cover with water.

### Simmer
- Bring to a gentle boil, then reduce to low.
- Simmer uncovered for 1½–2 hours.

### Strain
- Remove solids and discard.
- Strain liquid through fine mesh.

### Cool & Store
- Let cool.
- Refrigerate up to 5 days or freeze up to 3 months.
