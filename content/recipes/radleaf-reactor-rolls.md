  - "2 tbsp canola oil"
  - "1 tsp sesame oil"
  - "2 tbsp fresh ginger, minced"
  - "3 garlic cloves, minced"
  - "170 g shiitake mushrooms, thinly sliced (6 oz)"
  - "680 g lean ground pork (1.5 lb)"
  - "180 g cooked jasmine rice (¾ cup cooked)"
  - "1 large carrot, shredded"
  - "4 scallions, chopped"
  - "¼ cup cilantro, chopped"
  - "1 tbsp soy sauce"
  - "1 tbsp rice wine vinegar"
  - "1 large head Napa cabbage, leaves separated and trimmed"
  - "¼ cup hoisin sauce"
  - "2 tbsp water"
  - "½ tsp kosher salt"
  - "½ tsp black pepper"

recipeInstructions:
  - "Activate the Filling: Heat canola oil and sesame oil in a skillet over medium. Add ginger, garlic, and mushrooms. Sauté 4–5 minutes until fragrant and softened. Remove from heat."
  - "Combine: In a large bowl, mix pork, cooked rice, carrot, scallions, cilantro, soy sauce, vinegar, salt, and pepper. Fold in the sautéed mushroom mixture."
  - "Prep the Leaves: Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Blanch cabbage leaves for 30–45 seconds until pliable. Pat dry."
  - "Roll: Place ⅓–½ cup filling near the base of each leaf. Roll tightly, tucking sides as needed. Place seam-side down."
  - "Glaze: Mix hoisin sauce and water. Brush lightly over the rolls."

  - "Instant Pot Method (Primary): Add 1 cup water to the pot. Place a trivet inside. Arrange rolls in a single layer (stack if needed). Pressure cook HIGH for 12 minutes. Natural release 5 minutes, then quick release. Optional: Air fry or broil 3–4 minutes to caramelize glaze."

  - "Oven Method (Backup): Place rolls seam-side down in a baking dish. Cover loosely with foil. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30–35 minutes. Uncover last 5 minutes to caramelize."

  - "Air Fryer Finish (Optional): Brush lightly with glaze and air fry at 380°F (193°C) for 4–6 minutes until edges crisp."

## Overview
After the Collapse, leafy wraps became a common way to stretch protein while keeping meals portable. These **Radleaf Reactor Rolls** follow that tradition: savory, compact, reheatable, and designed to feed a full Vault household without drama.

**Theme:** Surface Survival Wrap  
**Profile:** High-protein, moderate-carb, family-safe  
**Method:** Instant Pot primary + air fryer finish  
**Vibe:** Savory-sweet glaze, gingery aromatics, comfort food without chaos

## Vault Notes

### Kid / Adult Heat Split
**Base Batch (Kid-Safe):**  
Mild, savory, slightly sweet

**Adult Afterburn Options (Add Post-Plating):**  
- Chili crisp drizzle  
- Sriracha or sambal  
- Gochujang glaze  
- Red pepper flakes

Keeps one unified batch.

---

### Portion Control Adjustments
- **Lower carbs:** Reduce rice to **120 g cooked (½ cup)**  
- **Lower fat:** Use extra-lean ground pork or 50/50 pork + chicken  
- **More volume:** Add extra mushrooms or shredded zucchini

---

### Storage Protocol
**Fridge:** 4 days  
**Freezer:** 2 months (wrap individually)

**Best Reheat Methods:**
- **Air fryer:** 360°F (182°C), 5–6 min  
- **Microwave:** 90 seconds, covered  
- **Skillet:** Medium-low, covered, 5 minutes

---

### Timing Tips
If using a single cooker:

1. Pressure cook rolls  
2. Remove + glaze  
3. Crisp in air fryer or broiler  
4. Serve immediately
