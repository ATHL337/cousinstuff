## Ingredients

### Core
- 6 cups VT-07 Chicken Broth
- 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
- Egg noodles

### Vegetables
- Carrots, sliced
- Celery, sliced
- Onion, diced

### Seasoning
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Dried thyme or parsley

---

## Instructions

### Sauté Base
- In a pot, sauté carrots, celery, and onion until soft.

### Build Soup
- Add broth and bring to a simmer.

### Noodles In
- Add noodles and cook until tender.

### Protein Drop
- Stir in chicken.
- Simmer 5 minutes.

### Season
- Adjust salt and pepper.
- Serve hot.

---

## Overseer’s Notes
- Add a squeeze of lemon for brightness.
- Great for sick days and cold nights.
- Swap noodles for rice if needed.

## Pairings
- Crackers
- Toast
- Grilled cheese
