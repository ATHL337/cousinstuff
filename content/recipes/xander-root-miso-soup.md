## Ingredients

### Core
- Water or light broth
- Miso paste

### Optional
- Tofu cubes
- Seaweed
- Green onions
- Soy sauce (tiny amount)

---

## Instructions

### Warm
- Heat broth gently (no boiling)

### Miso
- Dissolve miso separately
- Stir back in

### Finish
- Add tofu/seaweed
- Top with green onions

---

## Overseer’s Notes
- Boiling miso dulls flavor.
- Great “I need soup now” option.
