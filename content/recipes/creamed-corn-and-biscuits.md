## Ingredients

### Creamed Corn
- Canned or frozen corn
- Butter
- Milk or cream
- Sugar (optional)
- Salt
- Black pepper

### Biscuits
- Store-bought or homemade biscuits

---

## Instructions

### Cream Corn
- Melt butter in saucepan.
- Add corn and milk.
- Simmer until thickened.
- Season lightly.

### Bake Biscuits
- Prepare according to package or recipe.

### Serve
- Spoon corn over split biscuits if desired.
