## Ingredients

### Core
- Tortilla chips
- Shredded cheddar or Mexican-blend cheese

### Toppings
- Jalapeños
- Black beans
- Salsa
- Sour cream
- Green onions

---

## Instructions

### Layer
- Spread chips on a foil-lined pan.
- Sprinkle cheese and toppings.

### Melt
- Bake at 400°F until cheese is fully melted.

### Finish
- Add cold toppings like sour cream and salsa.
