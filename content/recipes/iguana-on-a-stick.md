recipeIngredient:
  - "2.25 lb pork tenderloin, cut into 1 1/2-inch chunks"
  - "4 tbsp red wine vinegar"
  - "2 tbsp honey"
  - "2 tsp Worcestershire sauce"
  - "2 tsp smoked paprika"
  - "2 tsp dried oregano"
  - "1 tsp ground ginger"
  - "4 cloves garlic, minced"
  - "1 tsp black pepper"
  - "2 tbsp olive oil"
  - "Red onion chunks (optional)"
  - "Bell pepper squares (optional)"
  - "Pineapple (optional, for sweet contrast)"

recipeInstructions:
  - "Marinate: Whisk marinade ingredients. Add pork to a bag or bowl, pour marinade over, and refrigerate at least 2 hours (preferably overnight)."
  - "Skewer: Thread pork onto skewers, alternating with onion/pepper/pineapple if using."
  - "Air Fry: Preheat air fryer to 400°F (204°C). Cook skewers 10–12 minutes, flipping halfway, until charred and cooked through (145°F internal)."
  - "Finish: Rest 3 minutes before serving. If brushing with leftover marinade, boil it first."
