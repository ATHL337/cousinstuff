recipeIngredient:
  - "3 cups vanilla Greek yogurt (or high-protein yogurt)"
  - "2 cups Sugar Bombs–style cereal (or lightly sweetened corn flakes / puffed rice)"
  - "1 1/2 cups fresh berries (strawberries, blueberries, or mixed)"
  - "1/2 cup granola or crushed vanilla wafers (optional)"
  - "2–3 tbsp honey or maple syrup (to taste)"

recipeInstructions:
  - "Layer: Spoon yogurt into cups or bowls, add cereal, then fruit. Repeat layers until filled."
  - "Finish: Drizzle honey or syrup on top and sprinkle granola or extra cereal."
  - "Chill (Optional): Refrigerate 10–15 minutes for a firmer texture."
