## Ingredients

### Core
- Ziti or penne pasta
- Marinara sauce
- Ricotta cheese
- Mozzarella cheese
- Parmesan cheese

### Optional Protein
- Italian sausage or ground beef

### Seasoning
- Italian seasoning
- Salt
- Black pepper

---

## Instructions

### Cook Pasta
- Boil pasta until just al dente.
- Drain.

### Assemble
- Layer pasta, sauce, ricotta, and mozzarella.

### Bake
- Cover with foil and bake at 350°F.
- Remove foil and bake until bubbly and browned.
