## Ingredients

### Core
- Turkey breast (bone-in or boneless)
- Unsalted butter, softened

### Seasoning & Herbs
- Fresh rosemary, chopped
- Fresh thyme, chopped
- Garlic, minced
- Salt
- Black pepper

---

## Instructions

### Prep
- Preheat oven to 350°F (177°C).
- Pat turkey dry with paper towels.

### Butter Coat
- Mix butter, herbs, garlic, salt, and pepper.
- Rub mixture all over turkey, including under the skin if possible.

### Roast
- Place turkey in roasting pan.
- Roast until internal temp reaches 165°F (74°C).

### Rest
- Rest 10–15 minutes before slicing.

---

## Overseer’s Notes
- Resting is mandatory for juice retention.
- Great alternative to full turkey.
- Save drippings for gravy.

## Pairings
- Mashed potatoes
- Green beans
- Cranberry sauce
