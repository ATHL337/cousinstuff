## Ingredients

### Core
- Ground beef
- Breadcrumbs
- Egg
- Onion, minced
- Garlic, minced

### Gravy
- Beef broth
- Worcestershire sauce
- Flour or cornstarch

### Seasoning
- Salt
- Black pepper

---

## Instructions

### Form Patties
- Mix beef, breadcrumbs, egg, onion, garlic, salt, and pepper.
- Shape into oval patties.

### Sear
- Brown patties in skillet; remove.

### Build Gravy
- Add broth and Worcestershire.
- Thicken with flour slurry.

### Simmer
- Return patties to gravy.
- Simmer 20–25 minutes.

---

## Overseer’s Notes
- Serve with mashed potatoes for full effect.
- Freeze well for later deployment.
- Swap beef for turkey if desired.

## Pairings
- Mashed potatoes
- Peas
- Dinner rolls
