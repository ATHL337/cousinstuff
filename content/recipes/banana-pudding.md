## Ingredients

### Core
- Vanilla pudding (prepared)
- Sweetened condensed milk
- Heavy cream or whipped topping

### Layers
- Vanilla wafers
- Fresh bananas, sliced

---

## Instructions

### Mix
- Combine pudding and condensed milk.
- Fold in whipped topping.

### Layer
- In a dish, layer wafers, bananas, and cream mixture.

### Chill
- Refrigerate at least 2 hours before serving.

---

## Overseer’s Notes
- Best served cold.
- Add crushed wafers on top for texture.
- Will soften overnight (in a good way).
