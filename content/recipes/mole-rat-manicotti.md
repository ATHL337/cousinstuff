## Ingredients

### Core
- Manicotti shells
- Ground beef or sausage
- Ricotta cheese
- Mozzarella cheese
- Egg
- Marinara sauce

### Seasoning
- Italian seasoning
- Garlic powder
- Salt
- Black pepper

---

## Instructions

### Prep Filling
- Mix meat, ricotta, egg, seasoning, salt, and pepper.

### Stuff Shells
- Fill manicotti with mixture.

### Assemble
- Spread sauce in baking dish.
- Arrange stuffed shells.
- Cover with more sauce and mozzarella.

### Bake
- Cover with foil.
- Bake until bubbly and cooked through.

---

## Overseer’s Notes
- Make ahead and refrigerate before baking.
- Freezes extremely well.
- Add spinach for nutrition boost.

## Pairings
- Garlic bread
- Caesar salad
