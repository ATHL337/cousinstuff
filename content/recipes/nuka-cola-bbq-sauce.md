## Ingredients

### Core
- Cola (regular, not diet)
- Ketchup
- Brown sugar
- Apple cider vinegar

### Seasoning
- Smoked paprika
- Garlic powder
- Onion powder
- Black pepper

---

## Instructions

### Combine
- Add all ingredients to saucepan.

### Simmer
- Bring to low boil, then reduce heat.
- Simmer until thickened.

### Cool
- Remove from heat and cool before storing.

---

## Overseer’s Notes
- Great on chicken, ribs, or burgers.
- Keeps 2 weeks refrigerated.
- Add cayenne for heat.

## Pairings
- VT-10 Salisbury Steak
- Grilled chicken
- Roasted vegetables
