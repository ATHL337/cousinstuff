## Ingredients

### Core
- Elbow macaroni
- Butter
- All-purpose flour
- Milk
- Sharp cheddar cheese, shredded

### Seasoning
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Paprika (optional)

---

## Instructions

### Cook Pasta
- Boil macaroni in salted water until al dente.
- Drain and set aside.

### Make Sauce
- Melt butter in saucepan.
- Whisk in flour; cook 1 minute.
- Slowly whisk in milk until smooth.
- Add cheese and stir until melted.

### Combine
- Add pasta to sauce and toss to coat.

### Serve
- Season to taste and serve hot.

---

## Overseer’s Notes
- Add a breadcrumb topping and bake for a crispy variant.
- Mix in broccoli or peas for veggie intake.
- Reheats well with a splash of milk.
