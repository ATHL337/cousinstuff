## Ingredients

### Core
- Cooked chicken, shredded
- Chicken broth
- Milk
- Butter
- All-purpose flour

### Vegetables
- Carrots, sliced
- Celery, sliced
- Onion, diced

### Seasoning
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Dried thyme

---

## Instructions

### Build Base
- Sauté vegetables in butter until softened.
- Add broth and bring to a simmer.

### Add Chicken
- Stir in shredded chicken.

### Dumpling Dough
- Mix flour, milk, salt, and pepper into a thick batter.

### Drop & Steam
- Drop spoonfuls of dough into simmering broth.
- Cover and cook until dumplings puff and set.

---

## Overseer’s Notes
- Keep the lid closed while dumplings cook.
- Thicker broth = more comfort.
- Add peas for color.

## Pairings
- Biscuits
- Simple salad
